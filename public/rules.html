Box Fight

Playing Instructions: 1. Use Clix's map code: 7620-0771-9529 if you both you and your opponent wish to play on another map you may. If your opponent loads up a map you don't like, leave, don't play as that will count. 2. To kill your opponents, you'll have limited materials and a small construct area. Build and edit intelligently. 3. Keep track of your points; the player who reaches the score limit first wins! You must win by 2 rounds 4. If you cannot agree on what weapons to use with your opponent, use the golden pump shotgun and the blue assault rifle. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Host is team 1 in-game while the opponents switch to Team 2. Unless you are knocked out, you cannot respawn in the middle of a round. This is to avoid players preventing siphon being awarded to their opponents.

Build Fight

Playing Instructions: 1. Use Raider's map code: 1832-0431-4852 ,if you both you and your opponent wish to play on another map you may. If your opponent loads up a map you don't like, leave, don't play as that will count. 2. In an open map against your opponent, you will have infinite materials to build with. As you compete for height and try to destroy your opponent, protect yourself. 3. Keep track of your points; the player who reaches the score limit first wins! You must win by 2 rounds. 4. If there's disagreements as to what weapons to use, please use a golden pump shotgun and a blue assault rifle. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- A round defeat will occur from stalling and camping in bases. 1. If you box up for more than 7-8 seconds, you will immediately lose, this is a high-intensity game mode. You may only build using wood. 1. A round will be lost if you use a different material. Setup 1. To begin press the activation button, then, both players must run into the wall, you can't stay further back and sprint to cross the barrier with any kind of momentum. 2. You must first ramp out; then, before shooting, you must be at least 1 layer above the maps pre-placed ramp. You are not allowed to build in the first 2 tiles above the maps pre-place ramps. Besides these rules, how you start is entirely up to you. 3. A round will be lost if you shoot before completing the setup. Buildings that have been reset 1. When you achieve maximum height, you must reset and start over with your current health. 2. When you start a new round, you must reset the builds. 3. Any additional time to reset the builds will result in a round loss. 4. Falling of the Build-fight doesn't warrant a restart, you must build back up, you are allowed to shoot your opponent who's falling off the buildfight or building up. At any given time, you may not break more than half of the construct fight down. 1. A round loss will occur if you break beyond 50%. Excessive and needless 90-ing is prohibited, and a round redo will be required. 1. The personnel at GamerMatch will carefully review and determine this. You are allowed to start by doing 90s as much as you want. However if you 90 for the sake of increasing the size of the buildfight your opponent is chopping your opponent may be awarded the round win.

General Rules:

In all matches, the admins and moderators have the last say. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- Platforms & Epic Names 1. If a player's platform or Epic Name is incorrect, the player has 7 minutes from the time a moderator enters the conversation to remedy the problem. 2. The game must be restarted if the player resolves the issue by switching accounts or platforms. 3. If a player changes their Epic Name on their GamerMatch account, the score remains the same and the game must be restarted. 4. Moderators have the option of allowing players to modify their Epic Name early if they like. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Continue to play if you're unsure. 1. Even if you think your opponent is breaking the rules, we urge finishing the game! 2. Continuing to play a game will never result in a penalty. 3. If your report is correct, the match will either be replayed or will end in your favor. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- AFK and availability 1. From the start of a match, all members get 15 minutes to prepare in the lobby. 2. To verify that the opposition team did not join within 15 minutes, chat and/or join log photo evidence will be necessary. 3. If any teammates are absent, the match may be played with fewer players. 4. Cancellation of the match must be agreed upon by both parties. While your opponent is AFK, you can only get one kill against them at a time. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Glitches in the game 1. If a game glitch occurs, the rounds in which the game glitch occurs are not counted. 2. Exploits and two-sided game mechanics that are repeatable do not count as game glitches. 3. A game crash isn't considered a bug because it's hard to know whether it caused intentionally or not. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Party rules, Host, Map & Region 1. The game is reset if the match is played in the wrong region. 2. If the host is incorrect, all completed matches count, and the non-hosting team has the option to quit and request the host after the current round. 3. If the map is incorrect, all completed matches are counted, and after the current round, the default GamerMatch recommended map can be picked. 4. It's an immediate round loss if you kick your opponent out of the party in the middle of the round. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Observing / Spectating 1. Any rounds with an outside spectator will be reset, and the team that did not invite the spectator will receive a point. 2.The lobby should be made as secluded as feasible. 3. It's important to note that other players joining the lobby but not spectating will not cause a redo. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Toxicity Use the !toxic command for more info in the discord server. You are not allowed to be overly toxic, this will result in a ban. However most forms of toxicity are fine, it's up to mods and admins to decide. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Disconnections 1. If a team member leaves the game (or respawns) after the round has begun, the remaining members must finish the round. That round would be declared a loss if all players left. 2. The round does not count if a team member leaves before the round begins, and the game is halted. 3. All unavailable players will be unable to play for the remainder of the game if the game is paused for more than 5 minutes. 4. Disconnecting due to lag, a player's game crashing, or any other equipment issues, though unpleasant, does not result in a redo because it is hard to show whether or not it was intentional. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Deception 1. Lying to your opponents regarding the rules, expectations, setup, or anything else that has a direct and clear impact on the match is not allowed. Although not advised, deceiving someone about your skill level is allowed. 2. The rule is upheld if enforceable and at least one person from each team agrees to an alternative or additional game rule or modification by voice or chat, with evidence. 3. Attempting to complete a game or report someone while one is in progress is considered deception. 4. You should immediately stop playing if you flag the match as complete or report your opponent. If you leave in the middle of a round and your report is invalid, the opposing side will receive a point, so if you're not sure, finish the match first.

Kill Race

Playing Instructions: 1. Join your opponents in a duos lobby (if 1v1) or a squads lobby (if 2v2). 2. Play a typical Fortnite match with 100 players. 3. Keep count of your kills; at the end of the series, the team with the most kills wins! If the kill score is tied, the side with the longest survival time wins. A fresh game will be played if both parties have the same number of kills and win the game. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- You may not inflict damage on your opponent(s) directly. 1. You are not allowed to cause your opponent to receive direct fall damage by demolishing their build(s). 2. You can't change or destroy your opponent's builds if doing so exposes them to enemy fire and causes damage to them. This is usually determined by whether or not damaging the construction resulted in them taking damage in the next 15 seconds. 3. You are allowed to do harm indirectly by stealing loot, and damaging vehicles. It is forbidden to interrupt healing or revivals. 1. Interrupting healing or revivals, even if unintentionally, can be game-breaking, hence it's not allowed. You are not allowed to destroy your opponent's launch pad while they are trying to use it. 1. It's oddly particular, but it's also bothersome for the person you're doing it to. 2. You may not obstruct your opponent's gameplay unduly. 3. Excessively demolishing your opponent's builds, boxing them up, or generally being intentionally unpleasant may result in a loss or ban. To pick up objects beneath a downed opponent, you must first pick them up. 1. It is not permitted to pick up your opponent for more than a second or two. 2. It is also forbidden to pick up your opponent in order to prevent their teammate from reviving them, to cause them to receive damage, or for any other reason. When you enter any sort of mobility item like a car then you understand that your opponent has the right to move you around as they wish. (NOTE: Theses rules will be available directly on the website)

Realistic

Playing Instructions 1. Please use Pandvil's map: 6570-5231-1418 if you both you and your opponent wish to play on another map you may. If your opponent loads up a map you don't like, leave, don't play as that will count. 2. You'll spawn in a random location on the map with random loot and limited resources to build, kill, and defend against your opponents. 3. Keep track of your points; the player who reaches the score limit first wins! You must win by a margin of two points. 4. If you cannot agree on which option to pick for the loot please play with "same everything". ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Host is team 1 in-game while the opponents switch to Team 2. 1. All points awarded to the incorrect teams stand. Unless you are knocked out, you cannot respawn in the middle of a round. 1. This is to avoid offering siphon by preventing respawning. If a round ever ends while both teams are still alive, the team with the most surviving players wins. 2. If both teams are still alive at the end of a round, the team with the most surviving players wins, this rule is only applicable to maps with a countdown timer. If you end the game / back to hub after the loot has been distributed, you will lose the round.
